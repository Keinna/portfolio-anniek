I am an enthusiastic developer with a good sense of humor. I like to create projects that look attractive and have great user experience at the same time. I love how you can turn a design into something you can interact with. My goal is to continually improve my coding skills. I strive to write code that is reusable and easy to maintain. I pursued my studies in psychomotor therapy at Windesheim University of Applied Sciences in Zwolle. Following my graduation, I gained professional experience by working at various mental health organizations, including RIBW K/AM (Institution for Protected and Assisted Living) and Cordaan.

In my capacity as a team leader at the RIBW, I was tasked with reviewing and evaluating multiple processes within the team with the aim of enhancing the quality of care provided to our clients. Additionally, I provided guidance and support to my colleagues in their work responsibilities.

After working in the field of mental health care for over 10 years, I desired something different. I sought an opportunity that would allow me to combine my creativity with my passion for acquiring new knowledge. This led me to enroll in a web development course.

From the moment I began coding, I realized I had made the correct decision.

Currently, I am employed at a digital agency located in Alkmaar, the Netherlands. Over the past years, my work has primarily involved constructing websites and developing various features for a diverse range of clients, each with unique requirements. These projects have varied in scale, spanning from small-scale projects to large-scale projects. In order to successfully execute these projects, I have collaborated closely with numerous developers, designers, and project managers within a Scrum team, utilizing PHP for backend development.

This year, I have taken the modules Software Systems (in Java) and Network Systems at the University of Twente.
